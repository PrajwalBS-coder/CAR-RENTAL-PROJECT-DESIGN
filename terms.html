Car Segments

We try to show to most relevant car on fleet coming from the car rental company online service. When you are making a reservation, you reserve the car class, not the exact car. This is a standard in car rental industry. Please note that car rental companies do not guarantee exact make and model except for high-end cars (Ferrari, etc) or if it specifically states that it is a “guranteed model”. When picking up the car, you will receive a similar or a better segment car. Similar car means any car brand in the same car class. For example, if you reserve intermediate car you will receive Toyota Corolla, Hyundai Elantra or a similar class car but not a Hyundai Accent which is an economy car.
